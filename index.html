U.S. Securities Exchange Commision (SEC) publishes all forms submitted by firms whose securities are publicly traded on its EDGAR platform. Particularly, firms that wish to file for an initial public offering have to file prospectuses pursuant to filing rule 424B4. These prospectuses lay out key information on the offering, including the amount of capital raised, the sector of the company, underwriters for the offering, and their commissions.
