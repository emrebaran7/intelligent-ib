Intelligent IB

Intelligent IB is a data visualizer designed for investment banks to monitor initial public offering (IPO) activity in the U.S.

U.S. Securities Exchange Commision (SEC) publishes all forms submitted by firms whose securities are publicly traded on its EDGAR platform. Firms that wish to file for an IPO have to file prospectuses pursuant to filing rule 424B4. These prospectuses lay out key information on the offering, including the amount of capital raised, the sector of the company, underwriters for the offering, and their commissions. All of the displayed data is sourced from these filings.

Sectors are based on North American Industry Classification System (NAICS). Fees only include underwriting commissions and are in USD millions.
